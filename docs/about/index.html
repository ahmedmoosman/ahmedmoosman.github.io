Ciao

I am a Research Assistant at the University of Trento, working on slum detection in Africa and integrating earth observation data into quantitative economics to better understand the dynamics of slum evolution and inform more effective intervention policies to address informal settlements.

Additionally, I occasionally work as a Industrial Development Consultant at the United Nations Industrial Development Organization, focusing on industrial research, policy evaluation, and predictions using economic complexity tools.

Learn more about my research interest in publications.

About me
